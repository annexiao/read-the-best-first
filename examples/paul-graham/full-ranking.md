# Ranking record: Paul Graham, 230 essays

Scored 2026-07-09 by claude-opus (6 parallel batch judges, ~40 essays each), judging first ~450 words of each essay.

**Rubric:** Dimensions, each 0-10: insight (something true you had never articulated), contrarian depth (challenges a broadly held belief with substance), philosophical reach (generalizes to how you think or live). Overall 1-10 is the judge's holistic call informed by the dimensions, not their average. Overall anchors: 9-10 worldview-shifting; 7-8 strong original framework, high transfer; 5-6 solid but topic-bounded; 3-4 competent but conventional or era-bound; 1-2 announcements/occasional pieces.

**Article gate:** 232 pieces fetched; 2 excluded before the book was built (non-article stubs under the ~500-char body floor): RSS (10 words), Lisp for Web-Based Applications (55 words)
